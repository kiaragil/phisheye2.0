UniTea
SOFTWARE DEV/WEB DEV

UniTea is a web app developed for a final group project at San Fracnisco State University. It's purpose was to promote university study groups in a post-COVID setting. The site has forum-style posting. Users could make accounts, make and interact with posts, and even customize their profile.

I acted as a member of the back-end team and as project lead. On the back end, my duties were developing the search function and the icon upload feature. As project lead, my responsibilities included delegating roles and tasks, managing the synchronicity of the team, and ultimately delivering a product that met the expected deadlines and deliverables. The stack used Python, Django, JavaScript, Materialize CSS, and Bootstrap. It was hosted using AWS EC2.
